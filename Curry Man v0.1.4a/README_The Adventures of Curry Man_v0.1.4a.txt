Overview:
----------
Curry Man is an absurdist 2.5D superhero side-scrolling action game inspired by
classic Kamen Rider heroes and pro wrestling theatrics. Developed as a love
letter to old-school beat-em-ups, it combines Indian humor, stylized visuals,
and over-the-top combat for a uniquely spicy experience.

----------
Move .............. WASD

Punch Combo ....... Left Mouse Button
Kick Combo ........ Right Mouse Button
Super Move 1 ...... Q
Super Move 2 ...... E

Gameplay Summary:
-----------------
- Chain punches and kicks to build your combo meter. 
- 3 punches and 3 kicks are included in the game, all of which can be accessed with a single click, double click and triple click, allowing you to choose the order of your combo.
- Every set number successful hits charges the Super Meter.
- Supers unleash ridiculous finishing moves.
- KO all enemies to trigger a fade-to-black and automatic restart.

Audio:
------
- Background ambient track loops seamlessly through gameplay and restarts.
- Punch, kick, and super sound effects synced to animation timings.
- AudioManager system ensures clean transitions and no overlaps.

Level Design:
--------------
- Scene: "CurryCity Street" — a neon-lit Indian alleyway.
- Environment features streetlights, fences, vehicles, and props.
- Exaggerated lighting to enhance cinematic tone.

Known Issues:
--------------
- Some combo inputs may misfire under heavy input spamming.
- KO animations occasionally freeze when interrupted mid-combo.
- Puddle reflections removed for performance reasons.
